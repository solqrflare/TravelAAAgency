What the Brochure Omits
01

The Library

Antigua's public library operated for years from a building condemned after a 1974 earthquake. A sign read "Repairs are pending." The sign remained. The books rotted. The marina expanded.

02

The Labour

The hands that clean your room, press your sheets, and prepare your plate earn wages your nightly rate exceeds before breakfast. Their warmth is genuine. The conditions producing it are not yours to examine.

03

The Water

Antigua faces chronic freshwater scarcity. The resort pools are full. The lawns are green. The neighbourhoods beyond the resort gates are managed differently.

04

The Money

Tourism revenue concentrates. Foreign-owned resorts return profits to foreign shareholders. The majority of what you spend does not circulate through Antiguan hands. This is not an accident.
